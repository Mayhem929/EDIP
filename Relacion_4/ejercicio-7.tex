$$ P(A_2|B) = \dfrac{P(B|A_2)P(A_2)}{P(B|A_1)P(A_1)+P(B|A_2)P(A_2)+P(B|A_3)P(A_2)} = \dfrac{\frac{2}{21 \cdot 3}}{\frac{1}{3}(\frac{5}{84} + \frac{2}{21} + \frac{1}{8})} = \dfrac{16}{47}$$
